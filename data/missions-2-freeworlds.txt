# The idea of this is a storyline that allows you to pick assistant delegates for the FW.


mission "Wormholes 2.0"
	landing
	name "Suggestion for delegate finding."
	description "You are told in this mission that you need to think of finding other delegates."
	source Mons
	to offer
		has "Wormholes 1.3: done"
	on offer
		conversation
			`A Hai you don't recognize beckons you from across the room.`
			choice
				`	(Go over.)`
					goto reminder
				`	(Ignore the Hai.)`
			`	The Hai first calls to you, then rolls its eyes and walks over. "In my culture, it is considered disrespectful to ignore even a subordinate. Is it not so in your own ways as well?`
			choice
				`	"Yes, it is, I apologize."`
				`	"It is no such thing."`
			`	"That is most interesting." the Hai replies. "I understand there are similar concepts with the Avgi, and the Coalition, and it is especially strong with the Gegno. But no more of this, I could talk for hours about cultures and their nuances. I have a message to deliver."`
				goto main
			label reminder
			`	The Hai begins speaking immediately. "Good evening, Captain. I have a message to deliver."`
			label main
			`	"The work of a delegation cannot be done by one's self, you should be thinking of other delegates to work with. You have the privelege of selecting an auxiliary and an assistant representative. The auxiliary representative will have the same power as you if you cannot be reached, while the assistant does not have that privelege. There is little urgency, choose your companions wisely." The Hai turns and leaves before you can reply.`
				decline

mission "Wormholes 2.1.1"
	priority
	name "New friend asking for job recs"
	description "A new friend asks that you reccomend him for jobs you hear of after he retires from the FW navy."
	source
		government "Free Worlds"
	to offer
		has "Wormholes 2.0: declined"
		random < 80
	on offer
		conversation
			`	There is a bright sign advertising a new bar recently opened at the spaceport.`
			choice
				`	(Check it out.)`
					goto visit
				`	(Maybe later.)`
					defer
			label visit
			`	The bar is a pleasant little place in a corner of the main hall at the spaceport. It has beautiful woodwork and a cozy atmosphere with few screens or electronics at all. It is busy, but not crowded and there is room at the bar as well as the booths along the walls.`
			choice
				`	(Take a seat at the bar.)`
					goto bar
				`	(Sit down at a booth.)`
					goto booth
				`	(Leave.)`
					defer
			label bar
			`	You get up on the high stool and order a drink. A man wearing a Free Worlds uniform moves down the bar to the stool next to you.`
				goto askingleave
			label booth
			`	A man at the bar wearing a Free Worlds uniform watches you cross the room to an open booth, then he gets up and approaches your table.`
			label askingleave
			`"Do you mind if I take this seat?" He doesn't seem completely steady on his feet.`
			choice
				`	"Sure, have a seat."`
					goto sure
				`	"Go away."`
			`	"Oh well, I guess it wasn't worth the shot anyway." He turns and leaves the room.`
				defer
			label sure
			`	He takes a sip of the drink he brought with him before he speaks. "My name is James Kyle, we met extremely briefly right before the battle of Kornephoros in the war."`
			choice
				`	"Nice to meet you."`
				`	"Umm... Hi.`
			`	He leans back, throwing the rest of his drink to the back of his throat. He begins speaking again without any sign that he had swallowed. "I came over to beg your acquaintance, but also to ask a favor. I am retiring from the Navy soon and I was wondering if you had any knowledge of a position that might fit an experienced Free Worlds officer.?"`
			choice
				`	"I have nothing. Unless you want to join my crew."`
					goto crew
				`	"I don't have anything right now, but I'll keep you in mind if anything comes up."`
			`	"Thank you for that. Here is my card if you run across anything. I have to go now." He gets up and walks out, tossing a few wrinkled bills on the bar as he goes.`
				goto depart
			label crew
			`	He gives a wry laugh. "Ask me that in a year and we'll see if I'm desperate enough. Please keep me in mind at least, here is my card if you run across anything. I have to go now." He gets up and walks out, tossing a few wrinkled bills on the bar as he goes.`
			label depart
			`	You finish up your drink and follow him out the door in a few minutes.`
				decline
